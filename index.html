An exciting line-up of Events

CSS Battle

"Gear up for an electrifying ride into the world of design with CSS Battle! Unleash your creative coding wizardry as participants go head-to-head, pushing the limits of Cascading Style Sheets to create jaw-dropping, pixel-perfect masterpieces. Get ready to be amazed as the battle for the most awe-inspiring web designs unfolds in this adrenaline-packed event!"

Stock Up: Pseudo Stock Market

"Get ready to dive into the thrilling world of finance with Stock Up: Pseudo Stock Market! Brace yourself for an exhilarating ride as participants embark on a high-stakes journey, strategically trading pseudo-assets to climb the leaderboard. Join us for a heart-pounding experience where financial acumen meets strategic prowess in the ultimate battle for stock market supremacy!"

Blind Coding

"Prepare for a coding adventure like never before with Blind Coding! Immerse yourself in the excitement as participants push their programming skills to the limits in this blindfolded coding challenge. Witness the extraordinary as developers rely solely on their coding instincts and problem-solving prowess to create awe-inspiring solutions. Get ready to be amazed by the unexpected brilliance that unfolds in this thrilling and unpredictable coding spectacle!"

Technical Funfair

"Step into the whimsical realm of innovation and excitement at the Technical Funfair! Immerse yourself in a carnival of code, circuits, and creativity as participants showcase their technical prowess with a playful twist. From mind-bending hacks to futuristic creations, join us for an unforgettable journey where the boundaries of technology are reimagined, and the fun never stops at the Technical Funfair!"

Baithak: the talk show

"Welcome to Baithak: The Talk Show, where conversations take center stage and ideas come to life! Join us in an immersive experience where intellect meets entertainment, as thought leaders, innovators, and influencers engage in insightful discussions that captivate, inspire, and ignite new perspectives. Get ready to be part of an engaging discourse, where every Baithak episode promises to be a journey into the realms of knowledge, humor, and compelling storytelling."
